# Business-Performance-Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze loan business and credit performance across multiple states and branches. The dashboard monitors key KPIs including logins, sanctions, disbursements, pending cases, rejections, and DPD status. Advanced slicers, bookmarks, and drill-down capabilities enable quick, data-driven decision-making, supporting management with fast, accurate, and visually impactful insights.
Derived actionable insights by identifying top-performing regions, most profitable product categories, and key sales trends to support business growth and management decision-making.

Tools Used: Power BI, Excel, DAX, Power Query.
